Me

I'm a designer, videographer and photo retoucher, child of the 80's and generally a geek. I take the odd photo, I read more tweets than I send and, on occasion, I make videos.

I'm a native of Wicklow Town and have an ongoing interest in all things internets and do my best to keep up with it all (impossible task). I dabble in motion graphics and a bit of card magic, as well as having a penchant for tweaking and streamlining production workflows. I know. When I'm not being a geeky employee I spend the rest of my time being a geeky dad and husband.
