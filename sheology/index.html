Hello

Thank you for taking the time to take a look at my work. Below is a selection of video projects that I feel best represent my skills and talents.

Quality Milk Awards

A video project for Ornua (previously Irish Dairy Board) documenting finalists for the Quality Milk Awards. I was involved in concepting, pre-planning, storyboarding, shooting (1 of 2 shooters), audio capture (including interviewing), audio & video editing, colour correction, grading and delivery.




Nuggets of Gold

A video project for Ornua (previously Irish Dairy Board) launching their Nuggets of Gold campaign in conjunction with the Ballymaloe LitFest. I was involved in pre-planning, storyboarding, shooting (1 of 3 shooters), audio capture, audio & video editing, colour correction, grading and delivery.


Irish Farmers Journal

A selection of videos for the Irish Farmers Journal. I was involved in pre-planning, storyboarding, audio & video editing, colour correction, grading and delivery.
